# Daily Log - 2026-02-01

## 📈 Stock Analysis App - Major Feature Update

### What was done:
Added **"Why?" reasoning buttons** with significantly enhanced explanations to the Stock Analysis app.

### Changes:

#### 1. Three new expandable "Why?" sections
- **"Why this signal?"** - Appears under BUY/SELL/HOLD signal
- **"Why bullish?"** - In Bull Case section
- **"Why bearish?"** - In Bear Case section

#### 2. Signal Reasoning Improvements
**Before:** Short 3-4 line explanation  
**After:** Comprehensive breakdown including:

- **Score Breakdown with efficiency %**
  - Example: "RSI: 12/25 points (50% efficiency)"
  - Shows contribution of each indicator

- **Market Consensus**
  - Counts: "3 bullish / 0 bearish / 2 neutral signals"
  
- **Detailed indicator explanations:**
  - **RSI:** Explains oversold/overbought with historical context ("RSI below 40 sees reversals within 1-5 days in ~65% of cases")
  - **MACD:** What crossover means + typical duration ("typically continues for 3-7 days")
  - **Bollinger Bands:** Calculates potential % move from mean reversion ("would yield X% gain. Statistically, 70% of touches revert")
  - **Volume:** Institutional vs retail, confirmation vs noise

- **Concrete recommendations**
  - Example: "Wait for 76-81 score (clearer setup) or 56-51 (clear avoidance)"

#### 3. Bull/Bear Case - Probability-Aware Language

**Key Innovation:** Tone and wording now depends on which scenario is more likely.

**If Bull Case has higher probability (e.g., 60%):**
- Header: "Probability: 60% **(More likely scenario)**"
- Section title: "📈 **Why this scenario is favored:**"
- Verdict: "✅ X/5 technical factors support upside. Monte Carlo confirms this as the **primary scenario**."

**If Bull Case has lower probability (e.g., 43.6%):**
- Header: "Probability: 43.6% **(Less likely, but possible)**"
- Section title: "📊 **What would need to happen:**"
- Verdict: "⚠️ Only X/5 factors support upside. 43.6% probability makes this the **secondary scenario**. While possible, the odds favor the bear case."

**Prevents contradictions:**
- Before: Bear Case at 56% might say "strongly bearish"
- Now: Bear Case at 56% explains it's more likely BUT shows 0/5 technical bearish signals - explains it's Monte Carlo-driven, not technical

#### 4. Enhanced Factor Details

Each bullish/bearish factor now includes:
- **Statistical context:** "65% reversal rate", "70% of touches revert"
- **Time frames:** "3-7 days", "1-5 days"
- **Calculations:** Mean reversion potential, % moves
- **Explanations:** What it means, why it matters

**Example:**
```
**MACD Crossover:** Bullish momentum confirmed. Histogram at 1.03 
shows acceleration. Short-term trend now outpacing long-term, which 
typically continues for 3-7 days.
```

vs old version:
```
- Momentum Building: MACD crossed bullish (histogram: 1.03)
```

#### 5. Verdicts with Nuance

**Examples:**

For HOLD (66/100, Bear 56% / Bull 44%):
```
⚖️ Bottom Line: Not enough bullish confirmation to justify entry, 
but not bearish enough to avoid completely. Wait for 76-81 score 
(clearer setup) or 56-51 (clear avoidance). Patience beats forcing trades.
```

For Bear Case (More likely at 56%, but 0/5 bearish signals):
```
🚨 Verdict: 0/5 technical factors point to downside risk. Monte Carlo 
simulation (56.4% probability) confirms this as the primary scenario. 
Modest -1.5% downside, but still the more likely path. Consider waiting 
for better entry or tightening stop-losses.
```
