## 🎯 STAR-Methode (Master this!)

Für **jede** Story:

**S - Situation:** Kontext (Client, Industry, Challenge)  
**T - Task:** Deine Rolle, das Problem  
**A - Action:** Was DU konkret getan hast (nicht "wir")  
**R - Result:** Messbare Erfolge (Revenue, Time Saved, Risk Reduced)

**Beispiel:**

**Q: "Tell me about a challenging client engagement."**

**S:** "I worked with a global automotive OEM facing regulatory pressure from NIS2. They had 200+ suppliers with unknown cyber risk."

**T:** "As Lead, my task was to design and implement a third-party risk management program within 6 months."

**A:** "I built a cross-functional team of 8, developed a risk scoring framework using NIST CSF, automated assessment with ServiceNow, and personally presented to the CISO board monthly."

**R:** "We assessed 180 suppliers in 5 months, identified 12 critical risks, reduced overall supply chain risk score by 35%, and won a $1.2M follow-on engagement."

**Bereite 5-6 STAR Stories vor:**
1. Größtes Projekt (Revenue Impact)
2. Schwieriger Client (Conflict Resolution)
3. Technical Challenge (Problem Solving)
4. Team Leadership (People Management)
5. Business Development (Sales Win)
6. Innovation/Thought Leadership
